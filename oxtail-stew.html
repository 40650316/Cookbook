Oxtail Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds oxtail, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 can butter beans, drained and rinsed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the oxtail with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the oxtail and brown on all sides, about 8 minutes total. Remove the oxtail from the pot and set aside.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot and cook until the vegetables are soft and beginning to brown, about 10 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato paste and flour to the pot and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute.
  5. Add the beef broth, water, thyme, allspice, nutmeg, and bay leaf to the pot and stir to combine. Return the oxtail to the pot.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until the meat is very tender.
  7. Add the butter beans to the pot and cook for an additional 15 minutes, or until the beans are heated through.
  8. Remove the bay leaf and season the stew with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice or bread.
